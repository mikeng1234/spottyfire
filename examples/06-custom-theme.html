Custom Theme Example

Define your own branded theme by passing an object to SpottyFire.setTheme()

SpottyFire.setTheme({ name: 'genxcript', background: '#0a0f1a', panelBg: '#111827', panelBorder: '#1f2937', panelShadow: '0 4px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.4)', textPrimary: '#f3f4f6', textSecondary: '#9ca3af', accent: '#2563eb', marking: '#f59e0b', palette: ['#2563eb', '#7c3aed', '#059669', '#d97706', '#dc2626', '#0891b2'], });
